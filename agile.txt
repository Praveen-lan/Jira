Scrum Master Responsibilities:

• Facilitates Scrum Events:
The Scrum Master organizes and ensures smooth execution of meetings like Sprint Planning, Daily Scrum, Sprint Review, and Retrospective. 
This keeps the team aligned and focused on Sprint goals.
==============================================
• Removes Obstacles (Impediments):
They identify and resolve any issues or blockers that slow down the team’s progress. 
This helps developers work without interruptions and improves productivity.
======================================================

• Supports and Guides the Team:
The Scrum Master acts as a coach, helping team members understand and follow Agile principles and Scrum practices correctly. 
They encourage self-organization and continuous improvement.
============================

• Ensures Process Discipline:
They make sure the team follows Scrum rules, roles, and practices properly. 
This maintains structure and consistency in the development process.
==================================

• Acts as a Bridge (Communication):
The Scrum Master facilitates communication between the development team, Product Owner, and stakeholders. 
This ensures clarity in requirements and reduces misunderstandings.
=================================

• Promotes Continuous Improvement:
They encourage the team to learn from past Sprints through retrospectives and improve performance, quality, and efficiency in future Sprints.
==========================================

In short:
The Scrum Master is like a team guide  who clears the road, keeps everyone moving in the right direction, and ensures the journey (Sprint) is smooth and successful.
