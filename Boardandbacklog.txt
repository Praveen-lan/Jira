Board (JIRA):
The Board in JIRA is a visual tool that displays the current status of tasks in a workflow using columns like To Do, In Progress, and Done. 
It is mainly used during a Sprint to monitor ongoing work, track progress, and ensure tasks are moving smoothly through different stages.

Backlog (JIRA):
The Backlog in JIRA is a prioritized list of all tasks, user stories, bugs, and requirements that are planned for future development. 
It is used for planning and organizing work, where tasks are refined, estimated, and then selected for upcoming Sprints.

Difference:
The Board shows active work in progress during a Sprint, while the Backlog contains all upcoming and pending work that is yet to be started.
